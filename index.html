About Me

About Me

Hello, and welcome to my personal website. My name is Nguyen Le Hoang （グエン　レ　ホアン）, and I am a PhD student at Ritsumeikan University, Japan. My area of specialization is in Machine Learning and Robotics, which are two fascinating fields with a lot of potential.

This website serves as my online resume and portfolio, where you can learn more about my academic and professional background. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an active individual with a passion for sports like cycling, swimming, and football. I have also traveled extensively and enjoy exploring new cultures and meeting new people.

On this website, you will find a comprehensive overview of my education, work experience, and professional skills. You will also find a list of my publications, conference presentations, and academic achievements.

Whether you are an employer, colleague, or fellow academic, I hope that this website provides you with a clear understanding of my expertise and qualifications. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about my work, please do not hesitate to contact me. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website, and I look forward to hearing from you.
